Welcome to the JISC Activity Data Synthesis Project, supporting and synthesising the results of eight JISC Activity Data Projects

  • AEIOU Wales — Activity data to Enhance and Increase Open-access usage
    Aberystwyth University and partnersC
  • EVAD — Exposing VLE Activity Data
    University of Cambridge
  • LIDP — Library Impact Data Project
    University of Huddersfield and partners
  • STAR-Trak: NG — STAR-Trak Next Generation
    University of Leeds and partner IDS-SIG
  • AGtivity — Exploiting Access Grid Activity Data
    University of Manchester and partner JANET Access Grid Support Centre
  • SALT — Surfacing the Academic Long Tail
    University of Manchester and partner RLUK
  • RISE — Recommendations Improve the Search Experience
    Open University
  • UCAID — User Centric Integration of Activity Data
    Open University
  • EDINA — Using OpenURL Activity Data
    University of Edinburgh
